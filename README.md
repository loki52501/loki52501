What I Do
I design and ship AI-native systems that are reliable, observable, and actually useful. My background sits at the intersection of cybersecurity operations, applied machine learning, and cloud infrastructure — which means I don't just train models; I architect the pipelines, evaluate their failure modes, and deploy them where they can scale.
Currently pursuing my M.S. in Cybersecurity Operations and Technology at Penn State (graduating May 2026). I'm actively looking for roles where I can own the full lifecycle of intelligent systems — from prompt architecture and RAG pipelines to production-grade backend infrastructure and model safety guardrails.
Four things I bring to a team:
🏗️ AI Solutions Architecture — End-to-end system design across multi-provider LLM stacks, vector DBs, and browser-native AI products
🛡️ AI Reliability Engineering — Adversarial evaluation of reasoning faithfulness, sycophancy detection, and benchmark regression testing
🤖 Applied AI Engineering — Fine-tuning, evaluation harnesses, and prompt engineering for domain-specific tasks
⚙️ Software Engineering — Full-stack development, distributed systems, and cloud infrastructure (AWS, K8s, Docker)
